Welcome Haecia players!

By LucioNov 5, 2018

Greetings, everyone! We are in need of language-translators! Please read below for more information.

What is required, and what do I need to do?

Please check our discord to find out what is required. Language-translators will need to translate just a few things in Haecia. This may include item-names, settings text, etc.

How do I apply?

To apply as an language-translator please join our discord, and go to the #applications channel. Next, follow the language-translator application link!
