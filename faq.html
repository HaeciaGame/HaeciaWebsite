FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Haecia.

What engine is Haecia running on?

Haecia is currently running on Unreal Engine 4.

What is Haecia about?

Haecia is a fun survival game for everyone. Explore the world and survive from the Zombies.

When will Haecia be released?

Sometime in February, 2019.

How often is Haecia updated?

Haecia is updated about once a week. This many change due to development status, or we may release mini patches here and there. This could also change due to other issues.
