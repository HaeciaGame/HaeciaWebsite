Welcome Haecia players!

By LucioNov 5, 2018

Greetings, everyone! We would like to announce the official dates for Haecia's first alpha version(v1.0). Please read below for more information.

When will I be able to test?

Starting November 24, 2018. alpha-testers will be able to test. This was stated in the information video on our youtube.

When will the public be able to test?

Starting December 1, 2018. the public will be able to test. This was stated in the information video on our youtube.

To get the game you will need to join our discord and go to the download link to download the launcher. Next, download Haecia in the launcher.
